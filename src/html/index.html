Child Psychotherapy

Hello.
I'm Tania Druce, an Integrative Child Psycotherapist.
I offer counselling and arts based psychotherapy for children and adolescents.

Integrative child psychotherapy is a supportive and creative way of helping children and young people with difficulties that are troubling them in their lives.

It is a relational approach, using the relationship between therapist and client, to create a meaningful dialogue from which there is potential for personal growth and change to take place. Children are provided with a number of different art and play materials (such as paints, clay, sand, figurines, puppets and musical instruments) to help them express themselves and process difficult feelings, without having to rely on finding the right words. I also use body work, providing access to mindfulness and relaxation techniques, to support children in managing and regulating powerful emotions. While for a lot of children and young people play is a natural and preferred form of communication, some find having a safe space and trusted adult with whom to talk to most helpful. As such, there is no pressure for young people to use art or play materials and therapy is tailored to the unique needs of each individual young person, at a pace which feels comfortable for them.
